Who are We?

Vital Logistics & Transport is a professional freight dispatch firm providing an opportunity for Independent owner operators with authority and small fleet owners to run independently while providing maximum freight selection and highest level of support.​

As a brand new carrier who just received their MC Authority or planning to do so, it is very important to ensure that your truck or trucks are running consistently generating revenue every day for you and your business. However, as you may have already discovered many dispatch firms that will only work with carriers who have been in business for at least 6 month or more. There is a logical explanation for this. You see, there are a lot of brokers who will not take a chance by booking a load with a newly registered carrier and risking potential cargo loss or damaged. No worries, Vital Logistics and Transport has you covered and we will get you loaded, well connect with those shippers/brokers who will work with a newly registered carrier. Here at Vital Logistics & Transport we believe that everyone has to start somewhere!
